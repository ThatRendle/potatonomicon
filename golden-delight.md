# Golden Delight

## Summary
Golden Delight is an all-purpose Australian potato variety with yellow skin and cream flesh. It is a versatile cooking potato marketed in Australia under the "Brushed potato" label, where it is sold with soil still adhering to the skin. The variety suits multiple cooking methods and is available as certified organic.

## Agronomic notes
Golden Delight is a medium-sized oval tuber with yellow smooth skin and cream-coloured flesh. It has been described as a versatile all-purpose potato with moderate starch content, making it suitable for diverse growing and cooking applications. The variety is grown commercially in Australia and marketed both conventionally and in certified organic production systems. Specific agronomic characteristics such as maturity class, yield potential, disease resistance profiles, and dormancy duration are not documented in widely available sources.

## Culinary notes
Golden Delight is prized as an all-purpose potato that works well across multiple cooking methods. It is particularly suited to roasting, mashing, pan-frying, and stewing. The variety is also useful in soups and gratins. The cream flesh and moderate starch content give it a versatile texture that cooks well without becoming overly waxy or floury. In Australia, the variety is commonly sold with a thin layer of soil still attached (marketed as "Brushed potatoes"), preserving freshness and emphasizing its farm-fresh positioning.
